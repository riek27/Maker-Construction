FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, process, and how we can help bring your construction project to life.

What types of construction projects does MCC handle?

MCC handles a wide range of construction projects including residential buildings, commercial complexes, renovations and remodeling, civil works such as roads and bridges, and general maintenance and repair services. We also provide project management and consulting services.

How do I get a quote for my construction project?

Getting a quote is easy! You can click the "Get Quote" button on our website, call us at 0927 772 016, or email us at info@makerconstruction.com. Our team will assess your requirements and provide a detailed estimate tailored to your project needs.

Do you work on projects outside of Juba?

Yes! While we are based in Juba, South Sudan, we serve clients throughout the region and surrounding areas. Contact us to discuss your project location, and we'll work out the best approach for your construction needs.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity of the work. Small renovations may take a few weeks, while larger construction projects can take several months. We provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase and keep you updated throughout the process.

Does MCC offer maintenance services after project completion?

Absolutely! We offer comprehensive maintenance and repair services to keep your buildings in optimal condition. We also provide warranty support on our completed projects and are always available for follow-up consultations and service requests.

How do you ensure quality and safety on construction sites?

We implement strict quality control protocols and safety standards on all our projects. Our skilled workforce is trained in safety procedures, we use premium materials, and we conduct regular site inspections to ensure everything meets our high standards of quality and safety.
